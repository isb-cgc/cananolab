This is the manage protocols section. A protocol is a predefined procedural method used in the design and implementation of assays. For example, a protocol could describe the steps a laboratory used for characterizing nanomaterials. In this section, depending on your authorization level, you may submit a new protocol, or search for an existing protocol.
